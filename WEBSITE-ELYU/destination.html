Urbiztondo Beach

Overview

Urbiztondo Beach is nestled in the town of San Juan, La Union, known for its tranquil and serene atmosphere, making it a perfect spot for relaxation and simple beach activities. The beach features golden sand and is surrounded by clear blue waters, creating a beautiful backdrop for sunsets and beach activities like surfing, volleyball, and picnicking. Despite being a popular destination for surfers, it maintains a laid-back vibe, especially compared to more crowded beaches in the Philippines. The local community and vibrant cultural events add to its charm, making it a great spot for both adventurers and those seeking tranquility.

  • Catching La Union’s wave

    Before anything else, La Union’s popularity was primarily built upon the conduciveness of its beaches for surfing.

  • Evolving beach-party culture

    La Union used to be known for a simple off-the-grid living, but that’s now being eroded by a newer wave of beach-party culture

  • Sunset Watching

    Visitors can also enjoy strolling along the shoreline or simply relax and watch the sunset.

Kabsat

Overview

Set along the northern coast of La Union, Kabsat promises a warm haven of food, and drink, nestled in a point where heaving swells taper down into gentler waters.

A term that roughly means “sibling” in the Ilocano vernacular, Kabsat envelops you with the legendary Northern hospitality, capturing the storied spirit of San Juan’s surf town where the buzz of urbanity seems worlds away.

  • Grab a Bite

    Our diverse, hearty menu celebrates the freshest local ingredients to curate a dining experience that is both regional and universal in its deliciousness.

  • Savor a Drink (or more)

    We serve up our idea of happiness with our vast selection of carefully crafted cocktails and a robust assortment of local craft beer on tap.

  • Beachfront Sunset

    As the golden sun dips below the horizon, painting the sky in fiery hues of amber and rose, La Union's sunset transforms the beach into a breathtaking masterpiece—nature's daily reminder that even endings can be extraordinarily beautiful

Flotsam and Jetsam Hostel

Overview

We are an artsy social hostel run in a fun easy-going way by a group of longtime friends & family who love travel and travellers. Our guests are part of our fun-loving Flotsam Family. Everyone pitches in, helps out, and is keen to hang out with all sorts of cool folks. So if you're up for an informal friendly place that celebrates laughter and music and design and parties, come stay with us.

  • The Hipster Hostel

    This artisanal, hipster and “millennial” hostel hosts several bunks and rooms for people traveling solo or in groups.

  • Party Hub

    At night, Flotsam Jetsam transforms into a party hub, and all the lights sparkle creating this dreamy semi-romantic ambiance where you can pour your heart out.

  • Camp Avenue

    Travelers, locals and tourists visit these place to experience the moment of camping near the seashore where they get to chill in front of a bonfire while drinking their favorite cold drink. The place have little huts for a little privacy to rest. And also sometimes they play a movie near the seashore projected on big white board where everyone can view the movie played that night.
